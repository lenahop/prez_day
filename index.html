BARACK OBAMA

Obama was our 44th President of the United States. He was a democrat and the first ever African American president. He is married to Michelle Obama and has two daughters, Sasha and Malia.

ABRAHAM LINCOLN

The 16th President of the United States, Lincoln was president through the Civil War and famously abolished slavery with the 13th amendment to the Constitution in 1863.

THEODORE "TEDDY" ROOSEVELT

Teddy Roosevelt, the 26th President of the United States, is known for founding the National Parks Service, being related to Franklin D. Roosevelt, and being played by Robin Williams in the movie "Night at the Museum".
